# Confonda - Project Features

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the features available in the **Confonda** application, designed to streamline construction management and financial operations.

## 1. Dashboard
The **Dashboard** serves as the command center, offering a real-time snapshot of the company's financial health.
-   **Financial Overview:** View current bank balances, total incoming payments, and outgoing expenses at a glance.
-   **Visual Insights:** Interactive charts and graphs visualize financial trends, helping you make informed decisions.
-   **Alerts:** See immediate notifications for upcoming payments or critical financial events.

## 2. Treasury Management (Trésorerie)
Manage all aspects of your company's cash flow with precision.
-   **Bank Accounts:** Centralized management of all company bank accounts and their real-time balances.
-   **Check & Promissory Note Tracking:**
    -   Track issued checks and effects (promissory notes).
    -   Monitor status: In Circulation, Paid, or Unpaid.
-   **Transfers (Virements):** Manage internal and external bank transfers efficiently.
-   **Cash Availability (Mise à Disposition):** Track funds made available for specific purposes.
-   **Future Cash Flow:**
    -   **Payavenir:** Track planned future payments to ensure liquidity.
    -   **Recavenir:** Monitor expected future receipts to forecast income.
-   **Financial Situation:** A dedicated view to analyze the overall financial standing of the company at any moment.

## 3. Purchasing (Achats)
Streamline your procurement process from request to payment.
-   **Supplier Database:** Maintain a detailed directory of all suppliers and vendors.
-   **Internal Requests (Demande de Fourniture):** Employees can submit requests for materials or supplies needed for projects.
-   **Price Requests (Demande de Prix):** Generate and send requests for quotes to multiple suppliers to get the best prices.
-   **Purchase Orders (Bon de Commande):**
    -   Create formal purchase orders from approved requests.
    -   Generate professional PDF documents for orders.
-   **Petty Cash (Caisse):**
    -   Manage small daily expenses.
    -   Track requests, justifications, and receipts for petty cash usage.

## 4. Sales & Projects (Ventes & Chantiers)
Manage your revenue streams and construction projects.
-   **Client Management:** Keep a comprehensive record of all clients and their contact details.
-   **Project Management (Chantiers):**
    -   Create and manage construction sites/projects.
    -   Link projects to specific clients for accurate billing and tracking.
-   **Payment Tracking (Encaissement):** Record and monitor all payments received from clients against specific projects.

## 5. User Management
Secure and organized access for your team.
-   **Role-Based Access:**
    -   **GrandAdmin:** Full system access and configuration.
    -   **Admin:** Managerial access to most features.
    -   **User:** Restricted access for standard operational tasks.
-   **User Administration:** Easily add, update, or remove user accounts as your team changes.
